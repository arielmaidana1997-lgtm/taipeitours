Moments in Taipei

Hi, I'm Ariel

Your Independent Guide

I'm originally from Argentina, where I got my bachelor's degree and worked in banking for five years. Seeking new adventures, I moved to St. Petersburg, Russia, to complete my master's degree, and in September 2025, my journey brought me to Taipei to study Chinese.

As an international student and traveler, I know what it's like to arrive in a new city. I'm excited to share my discoveries with you and help you experience the vibrant culture, food, and life of Taipei. Come join me for a walk, and let's explore together!

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know.

How do I book a tour?

Simply fill out the booking form on this page or message me directly on WhatsApp/Telegram. I will confirm availability and send you a GetYourGuide link to pay securely online, or we can arrange for you to pay in cash when we meet.

What is your cancellation policy?

I offer a 100% refund for cancellations made up to 24 hours before the tour start time. I want your travel planning to be stress-free!

What payment methods do you accept?

You can pay securely online using credit cards via GetYourGuide, or you can choose to pay the total in cash on the day of our tour.

Are private tours available?

Yes! All my featured tours are private, and I also offer fully customized itineraries based on your specific interests.

What languages do you offer?

I conduct tours fluently in English, Spanish, and Russian.
