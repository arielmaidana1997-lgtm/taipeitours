Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know.

How do I book a tour?

Simply fill out the booking form on this page or message us directly on WhatsApp/Telegram. We will confirm availability and send you a secure payment link.

What is your cancellation policy?

We offer a 100% refund for cancellations made up to 24 hours before the tour start time. We want your travel planning to be stress-free!

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex), PayPal, and Apple Pay through our secure online gateway. We also accept cash on the day of the tour for remaining balances.

Are private tours available?

Yes! All our featured tours can be booked privately, and we also offer fully customized itineraries based on your specific interests.

What languages do you offer?

Currently, our expert guides conduct tours fluently in English, Spanish, and Mandarin.
